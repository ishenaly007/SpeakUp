      <ul>
        <li><i class="fas fa-apple-alt fruit-icon red"></i> Strawberries (Red)</li>
        <li><i class="fas fa-lemon fruit-icon orange"></i> Orange slices or Cantaloupe chunks (Orange)</li>
        <li><i class="fas fa-pepper-hot fruit-icon yellow"></i> Pineapple chunks or Banana slices (Yellow) <!-- Using pepper as a placeholder icon -->
        <li><i class="fas fa-leaf fruit-icon green"></i> Kiwi slices or Green Grapes (Green)</li>
        <li><i class="fas fa-seedling fruit-icon blue"></i> Blueberries (Blue) <!-- Using seedling as a placeholder icon -->
        <li><i class="fas fa-wine-glass-alt fruit-icon purple"></i> Purple Grapes (Purple) <!-- Using wine glass as a placeholder icon -->
        <li>Wooden skewers</li>
        <li>Optional: Yogurt or a fruit dip for serving</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="safety-note"><i class="fas fa-exclamation-triangle"></i> Always ask a grown-up for help when using skewers or cutting fruit!</p>
    </section>

    <section id="instructions">
      <h2><i class="fas fa-utensils"></i> Let's Make It! (Instructions)</h2>
      <ol>
        <li><strong>Wash Up!</strong> Wash your hands and all the fruits thoroughly.</li>
        <li><strong>Prepare the Fruit:</strong>
          <ul>
            <li>Hull the strawberries (remove the green tops). Cut larger ones in half.</li>
            <li>Peel and segment oranges, or cut cantaloupe into chunks.</li>
            <li>Cut pineapple into chunks. If using banana, slice it (maybe dip in a little lemon juice to prevent browning).</li>
            <li>Peel and slice kiwi, or wash grapes.</li>
            <li>Wash blueberries and purple grapes.</li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Thread the Skewers:</strong> Carefully slide the fruit pieces onto the skewers in rainbow order (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple). You can repeat the pattern if your skewers are long!</li>
        <li><strong>Chill (Optional):</strong> For an extra refreshing treat, you can chill the skewers in the fridge for about 15-20 minutes.</li>
        <li><strong>Serve and Enjoy!</strong> Arrange your beautiful Rainbow Fruit Skewers on a plate. Serve with yogurt or fruit dip if you like.</li>
      </ol>
    </section>

    <section id="tips-variations">
      <h2><i class="fas fa-lightbulb"></i> Tips & Fun Variations</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Different Shapes:</strong> Use small cookie cutters to make fun shapes out of melon or pineapple.</li>
        <li><strong>Add More Colors:</strong> What other fruits can you think of? Maybe some raspberries (pinkish-red) or blackberries (dark purple/black).</li>
        <li><strong>Drizzle Time:</strong> Melt a little chocolate (ask a grown-up for help!) and drizzle it over the fruit skewers for an extra treat.</li>
        <li><strong>Grill Them (Grown-up Job!):</strong> Some fruits like pineapple and peaches are delicious when grilled for a short time.</li>
      </ul>
    </section>
  </div>
  <p class="outro-cooking">Cooking is all about having fun and trying new things. Hope you enjoyed this colorful adventure!</p>
