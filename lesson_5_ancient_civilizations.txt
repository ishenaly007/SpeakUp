    <h2>Ancient Egypt: Land of Pharaohs and Pyramids</h2>
    <p>Along the fertile banks of the Nile, one of the world's most iconic civilizations flourished for millennia. The Egyptians are renowned for their pyramids, hieroglyphs, mummification, and a complex pantheon of gods.</p>

      <figure>
        <img src="placeholder_pyramids_of_giza.png" alt="Placeholder for the Pyramids of Giza">

      </figure>
      <figure>
        <img src="placeholder_hieroglyphs.png" alt="Placeholder for Egyptian hieroglyphs on a temple wall">
        <figcaption>Hieroglyphs were the formal writing system used in ancient Egypt.</figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>
    <p>Key aspects: Pharaohs as god-kings, advancements in mathematics and medicine, belief in an afterlife.</p>
  </section>

  <section id="ancient-rome">
    <h2>Ancient Rome: From Republic to Empire</h2>
    <p>What began as a small city-state in Italy grew into a vast empire that dominated the Mediterranean world. Rome is famous for its military might, engineering (aqueducts, roads), law, and Latin language.</p>
    <div class="civilization-feature">
      <img src="placeholder_colosseum.png" alt="Placeholder for the Colosseum in Rome">
      <div>
        <h3>The Colosseum</h3>
        <p>This massive amphitheater hosted gladiatorial contests and public spectacles, symbolizing Roman power and entertainment.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <p>Key aspects: Roman Republic, Roman Empire, influential legal systems, spread of Roman culture and language.</p>
  </section>

  <section id="maya-civilization">
    <h2>The Maya Civilization: Masters of Astronomy and Calendars</h2>
    <p>In Mesoamerica, the Maya developed a sophisticated civilization known for its art, architecture, mathematics, calendar, and astronomical system. They built impressive city-states with pyramids and observatories in the heart of the rainforest.</p>
    <div class="civilization-gallery">
      <figure>
        <img src="placeholder_mayan_pyramid_chichen_itza.png" alt="Placeholder for a Mayan pyramid like El Castillo at Chichen Itza">
        <figcaption>Mayan pyramids often served religious and astronomical purposes.</figcaption>
      </figure>
      <figure>
        <img src="placeholder_mayan_glyphs.png" alt="Placeholder for Mayan glyphs or codices">
        <figcaption>The Maya had a complex writing system, one of the few fully developed in the pre-Columbian Americas.</figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>
    <p>Key aspects: Advanced understanding of mathematics (including zero), intricate calendar systems, impressive city-states like Tikal and Palenque.</p>
  </section>

  <p class="outro-ancient">The achievements of these ancient civilizations continue to inspire and inform us, offering windows into our shared human past.</p>
